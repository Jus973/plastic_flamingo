Slow Page Information

This scenario tests the patience attribute of personas. Personas with patience < 0.4 may stop early after only 30-40% of the session timeout.

Personas likely to struggle with this page:

  • frustrated_exec (patience: 0.2)
  • anxious_newbie (patience: 0.3)
  • mobile_commuter (patience: 0.25)

Personas like power_user (patience: 0.9) or methodical_tester (patience: 0.95) will wait for the content.
